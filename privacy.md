## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **VOID** ("VOID", "we", "us", or "our").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Overview of Data Use

- VOID helps you map and organize your personal world, including places, people, projects, goals, assets, agenda items, notes, affirmations, and related connections.
- Your app data is stored locally on your device by default.
- If you purchase a premium subscription, your app data may be synced securely to the cloud using Firebase so you can back up your data and access it across devices.
- Authentication is handled through Apple Sign-In on iOS.
- Subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store and managed using RevenueCat.
- VOID does not display ads and does not sell your personal data.

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### A. Account and Authentication Information

To use the App, you may authenticate using Apple Sign-In. We collect:

- Your Apple sign-in identifier as processed through Firebase Authentication.
- Your email address and display name if Apple provides them and you choose to share them.
- Your subscription status, entitlement information, and related purchase metadata through RevenueCat.

We do not receive or store your Apple ID password.

### B. Personal Organization Data

VOID allows you to create, edit, and store personal information you choose to enter into the App, which may include:

- Profile information such as your name, initial, mission, and onboarding status.
- Places, addresses, map pins, countries visited, and location-based objects.
- People, relationship labels, groups, and connection information.
- Projects, goals, assets, events, agenda items, to-do items, affirmations, notes, and related metadata.
- Dates, times, check-ins, frequencies, and other organizational details you add.

This information is created by you and used to provide the App's core personal mapping, planning, and organization features.

### C. Location Data

If you grant location permission, VOID may access your device's approximate or precise foreground location to show your position, anchor your world on the map, and support location-based features.

Location permission is optional and can be changed at any time in your device settings. VOID does not access your location in the background.

### D. Contacts Data

If you grant contacts permission, VOID may access your device contacts so you can add people who matter to you more easily.

Contacts permission is optional. VOID only uses contacts to support user-initiated person-adding features and does not sell contact information.

### E. Notifications Data

If you enable notifications, VOID may request permission to schedule local reminders for agenda items or similar user-created items.

Notification permission is optional and can be changed in your device settings. VOID does not use notification permission for advertising.

### F. Device Security and Biometric Lock

VOID may use Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode through your operating system to protect access to your private app data.

VOID does not receive, collect, or store your biometric identifiers. Biometric authentication is handled by your device and operating system.

### G. Technical and Diagnostic Data

We may collect technical information needed to operate, secure, and improve the App, including:

- Device type and operating system.
- App version and basic diagnostic information.
- Firebase and RevenueCat identifiers used for authentication, cloud sync, subscription management, and entitlement verification.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide VOID's personal mapping, planning, organization, and reminder features.
- Save your data locally on your device.
- Sync and back up your data to Firebase for premium subscribers.
- Authenticate your account through Apple Sign-In and Firebase Authentication.
- Verify and manage subscription entitlements through RevenueCat and the Apple App Store.
- Protect access to your data using device-level biometric or passcode authentication when enabled.
- Improve reliability, troubleshoot issues, and maintain the App.

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

**Free Tier Users:** Your VOID data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, locally stored data may be permanently lost unless you have an active cloud sync backup.

**Premium Subscribers:** Your VOID data may be backed up and synced using Google Firebase infrastructure, including Firestore. Synced data is associated with your authenticated user account.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

---

## 6. Subscriptions and Payments

Subscriptions are processed securely by the Apple App Store. VOID uses RevenueCat to manage subscription entitlements and related purchase status.

We do not receive or store your payment card details.

---

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the App and related services.

- **Local Data:** You can remove local app data by deleting the App from your device.
- **Cloud Data:** If you have cloud-synced data, you can delete your account and associated cloud data from within the App settings. Account deletion removes your profile and synced app data from our Firebase database on a best-effort basis.
- **Subscription Records:** Purchase and subscription records may continue to be maintained by Apple and RevenueCat as required for billing, entitlement, fraud prevention, tax, accounting, and legal purposes.
